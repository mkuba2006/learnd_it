How Learnd.it Works

With Learnd.it, you can choose the type of class you want, whether it’s individual sessions, group lessons, or live lectures. Then, select a specific teacher from the available options and start accessing the knowledge you need.

1

Choose the type of Class

Decide whether you prefer one-on-one sessions, engaging group classes, or interactive live lectures.

2

Select Your desired Teacher

Browse through a diverse roster of experienced educators. Pick the one that best matches your learning style and goals to make the most out of your classes.

3

Schedule Your Class Effortlessly

Plan your learning sessions with just a few clicks. Our intuitive platform makes it simple to set up classes at times that work best for you.

4

Enjoy verified learning Sources

Gain knowledge from top-tier educators using verified and reliable sources. Enhance your learning experience with trusted and effective materials.
